What we cover
in every session

Step 01 — Website Review

We start with your website — as a visitor would

Before any data is pulled, we walk through your website the way a first-time tourist would. We look for what's missing, what's confusing, and what would stop someone from picking up the phone or making a reservation. What a tourist sees in the first thirty seconds determines whether they stay or bounce — we measure that gap precisely.

Step 02 — Review Velocity

We map how fast your competitors are growing

Star ratings tell you almost nothing. What actually matters is review velocity — how quickly your competitors are collecting new reviews compared to you. A business with 90 reviews getting 15 a month is a stronger competitor than one sitting on 300 reviews that haven't grown in two years. Google knows the difference. We measure exactly where you stand in that race, calculate what it takes to close the gap, and score your review momentum as one of the most heavily weighted dimensions of your overall visibility rating.

Step 03 — Category Audit

We audit how Google categorizes your business

Your Google Business Profile has a primary category and a set of secondary categories that determine which searches surface your listing in the Map Pack. Most businesses set these once during setup and never revisit them. We analyze the categories your highest-ranking competitors are using, identify the gaps in your own profile, and make corrections on the spot. GBP completeness and category accuracy account for a significant portion of your Trailhead Visibility Score — and it's one of the fastest dimensions to improve.

Step 04 — Citation Consistency

We trace your business across the web

Your business name, address, and phone number appear on dozens of directories across the internet — Yelp, TripAdvisor, Apple Maps, Facebook, and many others. When those listings don't match exactly, Google loses confidence in your information and your local rankings pay the price. We track down every inconsistency, score your citation health as part of your overall visibility assessment, and prioritize the fixes that carry the most weight with Google's local ranking signals.

Step 05 — AI Visibility

We check whether AI tools are recommending you

When someone asks ChatGPT, Google's AI Overview, or Perplexity for a cabin near Gatlinburg or a restaurant in Pigeon Forge, which businesses get mentioned? Increasingly, tourists are getting answers from AI before they ever see a search results page. GEO — Generative Engine Optimization — is one of the ten dimensions scored in your Trailhead Visibility Score, and it's the one most Sevier County businesses have never thought about. We assess your current AI visibility and document exactly what's influencing it.
