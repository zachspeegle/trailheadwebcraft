About Trailhead

Built by someone
who knows the terrain.

The businesses tourists find aren't always the best ones. They're the most visible ones. I started Trailhead WebCraft because the restaurants, cabins, shops, and attractions that make Sevier County worth visiting deserve to be found.

As a retired Naval Officer with an MBA, I've built high-pressure systems that have to work. That same discipline — precise, methodical, no-excuse execution — is what I bring to every website, every brand, every project. No vague promises. No confusing jargon. No agency bloat.

Every engagement starts with a clear read of the terrain, a plan that fits the conditions, and craft that holds up when the season gets busy.
