Common Questions

Straight Answers

Do I have to start with the Base Camp Assessment?
No. If you already have a clear picture of your local presence and want to move straight to ongoing work, we can do that. Most clients find the assessment worth it — it creates a shared baseline and avoids fixing the wrong things first.
What's the difference between The Map and The Guide?
The Map is ongoing optimization — I'm executing a defined scope of work each month. The Guide is full management — I'm proactively monitoring, adjusting, and making decisions without waiting for your input. The Guide is for operators who want to completely hand off this part of the business.
Are there long-term contracts for the retainers?
Initial commitment is three months. Local SEO takes time to move — results in the first 30 days are real but incomplete. After three months, retainers move to month-to-month. If it's not working, we'll both know, and I'll tell you before you have to ask.
Do you work with businesses outside Sevier County?
Occasionally, for tourism-adjacent businesses in neighboring counties. My focus is Sevier County because that's where my local market knowledge is sharpest. Deep beats wide in this specialty.
Will you guarantee ranking improvements?
No. Anyone guaranteeing specific rankings is telling you what you want to hear. What I guarantee is honest work, transparent reporting, and a clear explanation of every recommendation. Visibility improves when the fundamentals are right — that's what I build.
